SkyFest 2014 - 20th to 24th September!

Every year during the Southern Hemisphere Equinox the Kundalini Yoga Community assembles near the Cradle of Humankind to present South Africa's very own Spring Festival of Kundalini Yoga.

The theme for this year is "know the other person is you".

Meet old and new friends at a heart-warming four-day outdoor event of clean air, healthy food, early mornings, nurturing yoga, morning and afternoon workshops, evening program around the fire and camping under the stars whilst learning from South Africa’s great teachers steeped in the teachings of Yogi Bhajan.

With a great children’s program to introduce young ones to nature and yoga!

View more info on our Facebook page! Go to facebook.com/SkyFestAfrica

Venue

Sima Kade Bush Retreat, Magaliesberg:

Nestled between the Cradle of Humankind and the Magalies Mountains, less than 60km from Joburg, lies a beautiful secluded valley. The fresh water spring enlivens and sustains a unique eco-system of hundreds of indigenous tree species and a wide variety of animals.

Festival grounds and facilities:

Mountain side stage arena with event marquee, soft grass floors and amphitheatre terraces. Natural, organic and vegetarian food. Health drinks/foods, crafts and clothing stalls. Shaded camping areas, with choices of Riverside, Mountainside, Wooden Deck or Level Grass. Natural rock pools. Medical and security facilities/personnel. Clean showers and toilets. Shade relaxation areas and bonfire boma. Solar-powered lighting for paths and facilities. Hiking trails and views of the majestic Magaliesberg.

Info

What to know:

The spring water is clean, pure and drinkable. Hot water for showers will be rare. Safe, guarded parking at no extra cost. Lanseria is the closest domestic airport. there will be volunteers preparing the amazing vegetarian meals - langar style “free-food”. Donations are welcome. The site will be open from friday the 19th.

What to bring:

Tent, Plate/ Mug, Water Bottle (to fill up from the pure spring), cash to support the bazaar and purchase food, Yoga Gear including warm clothes for early mornings and evenings, Yoga Mat, Sun & Mozzie Protection, cozzie, walking Shoes, Sun Hat, Plastic bag for your trash, camping equipment (tents will be available for hire at the festival) and lastly your unique self to celebrate and share!

What not to bring:

Alcohol, cigarettes, illegal substances, fires, domestic animals, bad attitude.

Legal:

The event organisers do not accept responsibility for injury, death, loss or damage to persons and/or belongings.
